## Medium Description (Smithery/Glama/mcp.run)

Query 2,053 Finnish statutes — from the Tietosuojalaki and Kyberturvallisuuslaki to the Rikoslaki — directly from Claude, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible client. Full-text search across 15,710 provisions with EU cross-references.

Built by Ansvar Systems (ansvar.eu) — part of Ansvar Open Law, providing free structured access to legislation from 70+ jurisdictions worldwide.

This is a research tool, not legal advice. Verify critical citations against official sources.
